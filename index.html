Tabulary

Tabulary is a free browser extension that helps you save, organize, and find your open tabs. Sort them into unlimited boards and collections — no account, no size cap, nothing to configure.

Read the privacy policy
What it does

Built to stay out of your way.

🗂️ Boards, collections, and tags

Organize saved tabs however you like — drag and drop to reorder, tag and search across everything you've ever saved.

💾 Works fully offline

Everything lives on your device by default. No account required, no data sent anywhere unless you choose to sign in.

☁️ Optional free cloud backup

Sign in with Google to back up your tabs to your own Google Drive — private to you, never on a server we run.

📤 Your data, exportable anytime

Take everything with you as a plain JSON file whenever you want — import it back in, or into anything else.

No tracking. No ads. No selling your data.

Tabulary doesn't run its own servers for your tabs and doesn't analyze how you use it. See exactly what it stores and where.
